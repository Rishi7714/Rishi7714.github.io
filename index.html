My VAPT Methodology

I approach security assessments not just by executing tools, but by systematically tracking the Cyber Kill Chain and mapping vulnerabilities to the MITRE ATT&CK Framework to ensure defensive coverage and risk accuracy.

01

Reconnaissance

Passive and active mapping. Identifying attack surfaces via OSINT, subdomains, port scanning (Nmap/Masscan), and technology fingerprinting to gather initial intelligence.

02

Vulnerability Identification

Testing application inputs against the OWASP Testing Guide v4.2. Utilizing Burp Suite to uncover SQLi, XSS, and authentication bypass flaws.

03

Exploitation & Chaining

Manually verifying vulnerabilities with crafted payloads. Establishing multi-step attack chains (e.g., Auth Bypass + SQLi + Exfiltration) to demonstrate real-world impact.

04

Reporting & Remediation

Translating technical flaws into business risk. Providing CVSS v3.1 scores, risk matrices, and actionable secure coding fixes (e.g., parameterized queries, CSP).
